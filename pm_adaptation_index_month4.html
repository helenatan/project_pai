This week's signal

PM hiring picked up to 4,890 postings this week, 11% above the 4-week average and the highest weekly count since January. The AI penetration rate reached 41.3%, crossing the 40% threshold for the first time. More striking: AI PM job titles now represent 6.2% of all PM postings, tripling since tracking began 18 weeks ago. H2 has moved from "accumulating" to supported. The data now carries a clear direction: AI is not reducing PM demand — it is bifurcating the profession into those who can work at the boundary of AI systems and those who cannot.

Methodology & Limitations

Data sourced from Adzuna and JSearch APIs. LinkedIn excluded — no public API. AI keyword detection uses a curated list of 40 terms updated quarterly; presence, not intent, is detected. Sector classification uses a manually curated company lookup table; multi-sector companies assigned to primary sector. This tracks postings, not hires. All limitations documented in full at pmadaptationindex.com/methodology.
