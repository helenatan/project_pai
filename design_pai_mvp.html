Methodology & Limitations

US PM job postings, updated daily. AI detection uses keyword matching across 40 terms — passing mentions and requirements are both counted. Skill counts are today's openings; company counts are deduplicated 7-day postings. LinkedIn and internal career pages excluded. Tracks postings, not hires.
